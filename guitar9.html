Dorweiler custom build

Build #9

Black limba body with a laminated neck of black limba, mahogany, and wenge. Features a 24-fret Macassar ebony fretboard with malachite star inlays and HSS pickup configuration.

Maker's Note

Black limba has this incredible natural warmth and texture that I wanted to preserve, so I kept the finish minimal. The malachite star inlays against the dark Macassar ebony create an almost geological contrast - those green-blue mineral accents against raw wood grain. The laminated neck construction with black limba, mahogany, and wenge isn't just for looks; it adds torsional stability while keeping the aesthetic grounded in rich, dark tones. This build has an organic, earthy character that feels rooted and natural.
