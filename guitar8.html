Maker's Note

This was my first multiscale build, and it required rethinking a lot of the fretwork and bridge positioning. The fanned-fret layout (25.5" on the bass side, 25" on the treble) evens out string tension and improves intonation across the board. I paired black limba with a spalted/flame maple top for that organic-meets-refined aesthetic. The Tim Henson signature Fishmans are voiced for modern playing styles: crisp, articulate, great for clean tones and high-gain work. Both the volume and tone pots are push/pull, each activating coil-split on its respective pickup, so you can run either pickup in single-coil mode or both split simultaneously. Combined with the 3-way selector, this wiring scheme opens up a wide palette of tonal options. Hipshot hardware keeps everything stable despite the unconventional scale.
