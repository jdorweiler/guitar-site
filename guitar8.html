A multiscale (fanned-fret) guitar with spalted/flame maple top over black limba, 5-piece laminated neck, and wenge fretboard. Features Fishman Tim Henson signature pickups and 25.5"–25" scale length.

Maker's Note

This was my first multiscale build, and it required rethinking a lot of the fretwork and bridge positioning. The fanned-fret layout (25.5" on the bass side, 25" on the treble) evens out string tension and improves intonation across the board. I paired black limba with a spalted/flame maple top for that organic-meets-refined aesthetic. The Tim Henson signature Fishmans are voiced for modern playing styles - crisp, articulate, great for clean tones and high-gain work. Push/pull pots and a 5-way switch give you a ton of tonal options, while the Hipshot bridge and tuners keep everything stable despite the unconventional scale.
