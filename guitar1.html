Maker's Note

This guitar showcases classic PRS aesthetics with premium flame maple and a traditional red burst finish. The flamed maple was carefully selected for consistent figure across the entire top. Multiple layers of finish create depth that makes the flame appear to move as the guitar is viewed from different angles.
