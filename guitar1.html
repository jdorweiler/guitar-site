Maker's Note

The flame maple on this build was carefully selected for consistent figure across the entire top - no dead spots, just continuous movement. I applied the burst in multiple layers to create real depth: starting with deep burgundy at the edges and gradually transitioning to warm amber at the center. The finish technique makes the flame appear to shift and move as you view the guitar from different angles, bringing the figured wood to life.
