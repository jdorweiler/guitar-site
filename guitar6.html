Blue Burst Flame Maple

A PRS-style build featuring blue burst flame maple top, 5-piece laminated neck, and 24-fret ziricote fretboard with star and fish inlays. 25.375" scale with Skywood and Seymour Duncan pickups.

Available by inquiry

Body & construction

TopFlame maple — blue burst finish
BodyMahogany
Neck5-piece laminate (mahogany, wenge, sapele)
FretboardZiricote — 24 frets
BindingNatural wood binding
InlaysStar inlays; dyed maple bone fish at 12th fret
TunersHipshot locking tuners
Scale length25.375" — Fender-like brightness with Gibson-style bendability

Electronics

Bridge pickupSkywood Lincoln Ave
Neck pickupSeymour Duncan Fat Cat P-90

Special features

KnobsMilled from solid aluminum
