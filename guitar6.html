Dorweiler custom build

Build #6

A PRS-style build with a flame maple top in blue burst, a five-piece neck, and a ziricote fingerboard. At 25.375" scale, it balances Fender-like brightness with Gibson-style bendability. Skywood and Seymour Duncan pickups mix punch at the bridge with P-90 warmth at the neck.

Available by enquiry

Maker's Note

The blue burst finish lets the flame maple show through while the laminated neck, ziricote board, and star-and-fish inlay package give this guitar a strong visual identity. Aluminum knobs and Hipshot tuners round out the premium hardware story.
