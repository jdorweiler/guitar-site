RADICALLY DIFFERENT IDEAS

  • Blueprint: Technical drawing aesthetic - grid background, monospace font, looks like guitar plans
  • Ticket: Perforated edge, serial number, collectible feel - each card is numbered
  • Brutalist: Massive bold typography, minimal colors, very modern/edgy
  • Magazine: Editorial grid layout - vertical text, asymmetric blocks
  • Embossed: Simulates pressed/raised letters - subtle and tactile-looking
  • Polaroid: Vintage photo frame with your guitar - nostalgic, personal
  • Vinyl: Record grooves and center label - music connection, unique shape concept

WHAT MAKES THESE DIFFERENT?

  • Not traditional layouts at all
  • Reference different design worlds (music, architecture, print)
  • Some could be die-cut shapes (vinyl record card)
  • Mix of vintage and ultra-modern
  • Conceptual rather than just "logo + contact info"
