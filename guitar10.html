Dorweiler custom build

Build #10

This build pairs a burl resin top with a laminated walnut, wenge, and maple back, plus a walnut, maple, and wenge neck. A 24.75" scale, Macassar ebony fretboard, Seymour Duncan JB/Jazz set, and Schaller wraparound bridge give it a classic-feeling but highly custom spec.

Available by enquiry

Body & construction

Body Burl resin top, walnut / wenge / maple back laminate
Neck Walnut, maple, wenge
Fretboard Macassar ebony with brass inlays and inlaid burl
Scale length 24.75"
Knobs Made from body offcuts

Electronics & hardware

Bridge pickup Seymour Duncan JB
Neck pickup Seymour Duncan Jazz
Bridge Schaller wraparound
Tuners Hipshot locking

Maker's Note

The contrast between the burl resin top and the walnut, wenge, and maple back laminate makes this one feel half natural artwork and half precision build. Brass-and-burl fretboard inlays, knobs cut from the body offcuts, and the JB/Jazz pairing tie the whole guitar together with a lot of custom detail without losing its classic 24.75"-scale feel.
