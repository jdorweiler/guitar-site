About the Workshop

I build guitars for players who want something they can't get off a rack. The kind of instruments that start with a conversation about wood, tone, and aesthetics, then get refined through collaboration until the design is exactly right.

Every build starts with wood selection. Figured maple, spalted varieties, exotic species. I source pieces that have the visual impact to carry a design. From there it's about the details: dialing in neck profiles, executing clean fretwork, applying finishes that enhance rather than hide the wood, and setting up the instrument to play the way it should.

The portfolio spans traditional designs, experimental resin work, and modern multiscale builds. Each one is built to be played, not just displayed.
