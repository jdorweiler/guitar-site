A double-cutaway with flame maple and ash body, 13-piece laminated neck, and birdseye maple fretboard. Features Lollar Dirty Blond single-coils (S-S-S), Wilkinson floating trem, and tobacco burst finish.

Maker's Note

This build leans hard into classic S-style territory - three single coils, vintage voicing, double-cutaway contours - but with some serious wood choices. That 13-piece neck (roasted maple, maple, wenge, walnut, mahogany) is as much about stability as it is visual drama. Birdseye maple for the fretboard adds another layer of figure, while the tobacco burst over flame maple and ash brings out all the grain movement. The Lollar Dirty Blond set keeps it vintage-correct tonally, while the Wilkinson trem and Hipshot locking tuners handle stability and tuning under heavy use.
