Dorweiler custom build

Build #7

Flame maple and ash body in a tobacco burst finish with a thirteen-piece laminated neck in roasted maple, maple, wenge, walnut, and mahogany, plus a birdseye maple fretboard. Lollar Dirty Blond single-coils in an S-S-S layout, a Wilkinson floating trem, and Hipshot open-gear locking tuners round out the package.

Maker's Note

This double-cut build leans into classic S-style tone and feel with a dramatic laminated neck, a birdseye maple fretboard, a flame maple and ash body, and a rich tobacco burst that shows off the figure. The Dirty Blond set keeps things vintage-voiced while the Wilkinson trem and Hipshot tuners stay stable under heavy use.
