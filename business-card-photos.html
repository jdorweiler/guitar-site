PHOTO APPROACHES THAT ACTUALLY WORK

  • Approach 1: 50/50 split - photo gets half the card, info gets the other half. Clean and balanced.
  • Approach 2: Subtle corner accent - photo is super faded in corner, doesn't compete with logo.
  • Approach 3: Photo strip on bottom - like a footer band showing your work.
  • Approach 4: Diagonal split - modern, dynamic, photo doesn't overpower.
  • Approach 5: Full bleed with info box - photo fills entire card but info sits in frame on top.
  • Approach 6: Ultra-subtle watermark - guitar barely visible, just adds texture.

WHY THESE WORK BETTER

  • Photos are composed for horizontal format (not rotated vertical guitars)
  • Logo and info always readable (proper contrast)
  • Photos support the design rather than fighting with it
  • Can easily swap in different guitar photos
