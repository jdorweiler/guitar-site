Maker's Note

The quilted maple on this build is AAA-grade with consistent figure across the entire top - no weak spots or dead zones. I applied the amber burst in multiple thin layers rather than one heavy coat; this technique enhances the natural depth of the quilting and creates that three-dimensional effect where the wood appears to move and shimmer as you change your viewing angle. The gold hardware complements the warm amber tones and gives it a classic, premium aesthetic.
