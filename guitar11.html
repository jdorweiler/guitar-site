Maker's Note

Build #11 takes the multiscale concept from #8 and refines it with a laminated body construction - flame maple top over black limba back, with a walnut core running mahogany and oak laminate stripes for visual contrast. The 25.5"-25" fanned fret layout improves string tension and intonation, while the pearl fretboard inlays add a refined touch. Currently working through assembly and detail stages before finishing begins. This page will evolve as the guitar moves closer to completion.
