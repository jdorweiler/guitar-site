Maker's Note

This experimental build pushed the boundaries of traditional guitar making by incorporating epoxy resin art techniques. The quilted maple body was carefully cut to accommodate a flowing blue resin "river" running through the center, creating a striking visual effect. The transparent blue resin allowed light to pass through, giving the guitar an ethereal quality. This piece found its home quickly and represents the kind of unique, artistic builds I love to create.
